# HR Policy Manual — Acme SMB Co.

## 1. Employment At-Will
All employees are employed on an at-will basis unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.

## 2. Work Hours
Standard hours are Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:00 PM local time. Flexible arrangements require manager approval.

## 3. Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 15 days PTO per year for the first 3 years, increasing to 20 days thereafter.
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal.
- Unused PTO beyond 5 days does not roll over at year end.

## 4. Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, treat colleagues with respect, and avoid conflicts of interest. Harassment or discrimination of any kind is grounds for immediate termination.

## 5. Performance Reviews
Annual reviews are conducted each December. Mid-year check-ins occur in June. Ratings range from 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Exceptional).

## 6. Remote Work Policy
Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Employees must maintain a secure, dedicated workspace and adhere to the IT Security Policy.

## 7. Disciplinary Process
Issues are handled through a progressive discipline process: verbal warning → written warning → performance improvement plan → termination. Severe violations may bypass earlier steps.
