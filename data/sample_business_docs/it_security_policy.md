# IT Security Policy — Acme SMB Co.

## 1. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who access Acme systems or data.

## 2. Access Control
- All accounts must use strong, unique passwords (minimum 14 characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols).
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all cloud services and VPN.
- Access follows the principle of least privilege; requests must be approved by a department head.

## 3. Device Management
- Only company-issued or IT-approved personal devices may access internal systems.
- Full-disk encryption (BitLocker / FileVault) must be enabled on all endpoints.
- Automatic screen lock activates after 5 minutes of inactivity.

## 4. Data Handling
- Customer PII must never be stored in personal cloud storage (Google Drive personal, Dropbox personal, etc.).
- Sensitive data must be transmitted only over encrypted channels (TLS 1.2+).
- Data retention follows the schedule in the Data Retention Schedule document.

## 5. Incident Response
Report suspected security incidents to security@acmesmb.com within 1 hour of discovery. Do not attempt to investigate or remediate independently.

## 6. Acceptable Use
Company systems are for business purposes. Prohibited activities include: cryptocurrency mining, torrenting, accessing illegal content, or installing unauthorized software.

## 7. Vendor Management
All third-party vendors with data access must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) before integration. Annual security reviews are required.

## 8. Policy Violations
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and legal prosecution.
