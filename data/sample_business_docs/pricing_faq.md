# Pricing FAQ — Acme SMB Co.

## Q: What pricing plans do you offer?
We offer three tiers:
- **Starter** — $49/month: up to 5 users, 10 GB storage, email support.
- **Growth** — $149/month: up to 25 users, 100 GB storage, priority email + chat support.
- **Business** — $399/month: unlimited users, 1 TB storage, dedicated account manager, SLA guarantee.

## Q: Is there an annual discount?
Yes. Paying annually saves 20% compared to the monthly rate. Annual plans are billed upfront.

## Q: Do you offer a free trial?
All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

## Q: Can I change my plan mid-cycle?
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Q: What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH bank transfer for annual plans.

## Q: Are there setup fees?
No setup fees for Starter or Growth. Business plan includes optional onboarding services starting at $500.

## Q: What is your refund policy?
Monthly plans are non-refundable after the trial period. Annual plans are refundable within 30 days of purchase, minus a 10% processing fee.
