# Employee Onboarding SOP — Acme SMB Co.

## Purpose
Ensure every new hire has the tools, access, and context needed to be productive within their first two weeks.

## Before Day 1 (HR & IT)
- [ ] Send offer letter and complete I-9 / W-4 paperwork via DocuSign.
- [ ] Provision company laptop and ship to remote hires 3 business days before start date.
- [ ] Create accounts: company email, Slack, HR portal, project management tool.
- [ ] Assign onboarding buddy from the same team.

## Day 1
- [ ] Welcome call with HR (30 min): benefits overview, PTO system walkthrough.
- [ ] IT orientation (1 hr): device setup, VPN, password manager, MFA enrollment.
- [ ] Introduction to team lead and onboarding buddy.
- [ ] Review Employee Handbook and sign acknowledgment.

## Week 1
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training modules (Data Privacy, Anti-Harassment, IT Security).
- [ ] Shadow at least two team members on live customer interactions or project work.
- [ ] Set 30/60/90-day goals with manager.

## Week 2
- [ ] Gain independent access to core systems (CRM, billing, support ticketing).
- [ ] Complete first solo task with buddy review.
- [ ] End-of-week check-in with HR to surface any blockers.

## Completion
Onboarding is considered complete when the manager marks the checklist done in the HR portal, no later than day 14.
